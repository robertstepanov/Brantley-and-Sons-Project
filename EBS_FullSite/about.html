Who we are

Emory Brantley & Sons Termite and Pest Control Inc. has provided pest control services to customers in Pinellas County, Florida, since 1971. Emory, Billie, and Sean Brantley are available for you every day at their pest control company. They pride themselves in the personal manner in which they serve their customers.

We are a CTE (Commitment to Excellence) company, a recognition by Dow AgroSciences for our commitment to excel in the area of whole-structure fumigation. The CTE program requires training, equipment, and stewardship in addition to other requirements by the state. We have been a CTE-recognized company since its inception.

Emory Brantley & Sons Termite and Pest Control Inc is an authorized operator of the SentriconÂ® Termite Colony Elimination system. We were among the first in Pinellas County to become authorized operators and are proud of our work to eliminate hundreds of colonies of termites every year through this system.

Our company participates as instructors at the BCC School of Structural Fumigation and the University of Florida School of Structural Fumigation. We have helped with the development of the UF Fume Manual.

Emory Brantley & Sons Termite and Pest Control Inc teaches continuing-education courses in fumigation for industry professionals. We have participated in research and development with Key Chemical and Equipment, Dow AgroSciences, Ensystex II, and Spectros Instruments. In addition, we have provides consulting services to government agencies, universities, and companies in the US and Europe. We also manufacture our own clamps and specialty wrenches for the fumigation industry.

Our company is licensed in Florida in the categories of General Household Pest Control, Lawn and Ornamental, Termite and Wood Destroying Organisms, and Fumigation. We are also licensed for wildlife remediation. All of our employees have Florida identification cards for pest control. Our fumigation crew leaders are certified operators and special identification card holders. All of our technicians have current training records.

Emory Brantley & Sons Termite and Pest Control Inc is a Florida Corporation and an insured company that carries additional policies above the state requirements. We carry worker's compensation insurance and are a Florida Drug-Free Workplace. Our company is inspected by the state and the manufacturers every year and have never been actioned for violations.
