Who we are

Emory Brantley & Sons Termite and Pest Control Inc. has provided pest control services to customers in Pinellas County, Florida, since 1971. Emory, Billie, and Sean Brantley are available for you every day at their pest control company. They pride themselves in the personal manner in which they serve their customers.

Our company is licensed in Florida in the categories of General Household Pest Control, Lawn and Ornamental, Termite and Wood Destroying Organisms, and Fumigation. We are also licensed for wildlife remediation. All of our employees have Florida identification cards for pest control. Our fumigation crew leaders are certified operators and special identification card holders. All of our technicians have current training records.
